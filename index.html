My Incredible

Experience

of First Time Living in Another Country

The homecoming dance occurred during my fifth month in America. I started getting ready so early that by the time I was ready, I still had half an hour before my ride came. My mind was full of excitement, yet I was still so nervous that I even made up a list of topics that I could discuss with people.

My friends came pick me up and we headed to dinner. Beyond my imagination, everything was going smoothly. Then, a girl who I recognized from school approached our table. I wanted to talk to her so I started with a compliment, I like your dress. She replied, Thanks, I like yours too. You look hot.

I thought, What does temperature have to do with my dress? I did not want to end our conversation though, so I quickly replied with confidence, Actually I am feeling little cold.. She looked confused and then suddenly gave a loud laugh. As my face turned red, she then explained to me that hot means looks good.
